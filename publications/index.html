Publications

Publications in reversed chronological order, assigned to one of the following topics:

  • Neuroscience-related:
    • BCI - brain-computer interfaces (classification of EEG patterns, online control, prediction of performance)
    • CRIT - criticality (long-range temporal correlations, 1/f slope)
    • HEART - heart-brain interactions (phase of the cardiac cycle, heartbeat-evoked responses)
  • LIT - analysis, visualization and automatic review of scientific literature
  • NANO - nanoparticle physics (growth, optical spectra, simulation of molecular dynamics)
